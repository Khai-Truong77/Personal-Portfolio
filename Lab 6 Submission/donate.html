The Jordan Thomas Foundation was created to help families with children who require prosthetics. Every child deserves the opporitunity to run, play and express themselves just like any other child, however not every child is blessed with financially stability. The Foundation helps children under 18 with obtaining and maintining the prosthetics they need to live a normal life. Over the course of 18 years, things like resizing, maintainance, and insurance would have cost a family over $150,000 for 1 limb. These high costs are preventing disabled children from enjoying the activies other kids might take for granted. As someone who broke his ankle and was helpless for 3 months, I undstand the joy and elation of being able to do something as basic as walking. My experiences have given me insight on how valuable a limb is. Help a kid regain confidence in themself and in their life by supporting the Jordan Thomas Foundation.
